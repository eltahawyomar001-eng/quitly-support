Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my progress?

Open the app to see your smoke-free timer, money saved, and health improvements. Your progress is automatically saved and synced to your account.

What happens if I slip up?

Don't worry! Use the "I Gave In" button to reset your timer and keep track of your longest streak. Quitting is a journey, and every attempt is progress.

How do subscriptions work?

Quitly Premium offers a 7-day free trial, then $4.99/month or $49.99/year. Cancel anytime through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Premium unlocks advanced tracking features and personalized insights.

How do I cancel my subscription?

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap Quitly
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Will I lose my data if I cancel Premium?

No! Your smoke-free timer, streaks, and basic tracking will always be available in the free version. Premium features like advanced analytics will be locked, but your core data remains safe.

Can I use Quitly offline?

Yes! Your timer and core features work offline. Data syncs when you reconnect to the internet.

How is my data protected?

We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage through Supabase. We never sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Force quit and restart the app
  • Check for updates in the App Store
  • Restart your iPhone
  • If issues persist, email us at omarrageh101@gmail.com with details
